What Is it? A high-protien, high-fat supplement that can be consumed directly from the package. SC+ includes fortified vegetable oil, eliminating the need for a separate monthly ration of oil. The updated formulation of USAID's CSB13, which includes a whey protein concentrate (WPC80) and an enhanced micronutrient premix. The USAID adaptation of World Food Programme's Super Cereal.
